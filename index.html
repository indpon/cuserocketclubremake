Welcome to the Syracuse Rocket Club

The Syracuse Rocket Club promotes and encourages safe hobby rocketry in the Central New York area

Next Club Meeting: The next meeting is Monday, August 19th at Walt's HobbyTown starting at 7:00 PM. The meeting will include a laser cutting demonstration.

New to model rocketry? Bring your rocketry projects or questions to our meeting - we'll provide answers, tips, and ideas to help you get ready to launch.

Next Sport Launch: The launch for Sat. Aug. 17 is CANCELED because rain is forecast for the entire weekend. The next launch is Sat. September 28, 2024 from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM at the
Syracuse Rocket Club launch site. The launch theme is Helicopter Recovery. The September contests are A-Impulse Helicopter Duration and 1/2A Glider Duration.

NOTICE: We are currently limited to flying low and mid-power rockets on our field. Rockets flown may not exceed G-impulse, 1500 grams (3.3 pounds) liftoff weight, 125 grams propellant weight, or 80 newtons of average thrust.

The Syracuse Rocket Club welcomes families and anyone interested in the hobby to attend our launches. We have rockets available for children to fly and take home. We will teach new flyers how to prep and fly their rockets. Join us at our next monthly club meeting or launch.
